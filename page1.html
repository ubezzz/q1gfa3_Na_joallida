Introduction

This paper shows the amount of time (in minutes) we spend with these different people. This covers how we spend time with family, friends, romantic partners, and alone. The objective is to help individuals recognize that hectic lifestyles and personal challenges can lead to the absence of meaningful moments with our dear ones.

The information is sourced from "Our World in Data" and illustrates the average amount of time that people in the United States typically spend with others between the ages of 15 and 75.
